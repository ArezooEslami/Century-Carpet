Century Carpet Grroup started its activity as a caroet supplier and also sales agent for carpet manufacturers such as Pardisan , Century , Farrahi and Patris in the spring of 1999 with the endeavors of a group of experts all exprienced in the area of hand-woven and machine-made carpets.
The aim of the group was creating a proper situation for carpet production.
Today , thanks to God, with a strong belife in creativity, research and innovation and enjoying the latest professional knowledge, we have managed to improve the quality and patterns of our hand-woven and machin-made carpets and provide the market with the premium products of Century Carpet in different sizes, patterns and colors for evry taste.
At present, the group has four carpet stores and showrooms in Mashad, a workshop for producing hand-woven carpets, and a carpet manufacturing factory; and the products of Carpet Century are distributed throughout the contry.
